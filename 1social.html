What are socials?

Exeter MCR socials are some of the most exciting gatherings in Oxford. Occasions include potluck dinners, exchanges with other colleges, and BOPs (the Oxford equivalent of a party). Most events are announced by email, but make sure to check the calendar below to stay up to date. Or conveniently sync it to your own calendar!

Events are organised by the social secretaries, and all MCR members are encouraged to join. If you are interested in organising a dinner exchange with Exeter MCR, please get in touch with one of the social secretaries.
